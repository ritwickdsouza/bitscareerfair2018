Why Exhibit ?

BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus hosts an annual career fair for its current enrolled students and Alumni. BITS Career fair is a great way for recruiters to meet talented BPDC students who are keen in technical, techno-commercial, consulting, sales, marketing jobs. Our career fair include opportunities primarily for full-time job positions but companies keen in hiring part-time and internship positions are also welcome to participate. Please register to book your booth at BITS Career Fair 2018.
