Why Visit ?

The Placement Division, BPDC is holding a Career Fair at the university on 2nd March 2017. This is the first Career Fair to be held at BPDC. The main focus of the event is on sharing information between organizations and job seekers (university graduates). It is a way to explore career opportunities within a variety of UAE based companies at one location.

We believe that the Career Fair is a unique event for students to meet and interact with various potential employers on campus.

Who should Attend ?

- Final Year Students
- Alumni
- 3rd Year Students (PSII Opportunities)
- 2nd Year Students (PSI Opportunities)
